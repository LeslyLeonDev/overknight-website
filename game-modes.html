Game Modes

Two Ways to Play

Overknight ships with two standalone experiences, both playable today in the current Publisher Preview build.

Mode 01

ThunderRush

A high-speed arcade racing mode built around momentum and control. Its standout feature is the rewind system — undo a crash mid-race instead of losing your run.

↻Rewind System

Undo a crash mid-race — a standout mechanic unique to ThunderRush.

🚗Multiple Cars

A growing garage, each vehicle with its own handling profile.

🏆Multiple Tracks

Race across a growing set of circuits and open routes.

🏆Free Play

Open sessions with no pressure, no timer.

⏱Time Attack

Race the clock and chase your own best lap.

🔥Drift Mode

Score-based drifting with multipliers for style and angle.

Mode 02

VelocityVentures

A fast-paced first-person shooter built for vertical movement across 20 weapons and 12 maps. Wall-run, slide and double jump through fights designed to reward speed as much as aim.

💩20 Weapons

A full arsenal, each with its own fire rate, damage and range.

🗺12 Maps

A dozen maps built for movement-first combat.

🏃Wall Running

Keep momentum along vertical surfaces.

🧍Sliding

Close distance fast, or duck under fire.

⬆Double Jumping

Extra air control for tighter movement lines.

🔘Semi Controller Support

Controller input is supported in gameplay today.
