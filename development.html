Development

Development Timeline

From a first idea to a public Pre-Alpha build — the order matters here, so this stays a straight chronological record.

March 5, 2024

The Beginning

The initial idea for Overknight is created.

2024

Early Prototyping

Early prototype development before settling on Godot. Different gameplay ideas and engines are tested during this period.

2025

Move to Godot

  • Development moves fully to Godot
  • Large optimisation improvements across the project
  • Driving system redesigned
  • Multiplayer systems begin development
  • UI redesign

December 2025

First Public Pre-Alpha

The first public Pre-Alpha testing phase opens on Google Play.

2026

Pre-Alpha Expansion

  • Major graphical improvements
  • Improved multiplayer stability
  • Improved driving physics
  • Large optimisation updates
  • More game content added
