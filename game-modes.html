Two Ways to Play

Overknight currently ships with two standalone experiences, each built around its own systems and maps.

ThunderRush — Vehicle & Track Artwork

Mode 01

ThunderRush

High-speed arcade racing built around momentum and control. Drift through corners, chain burnouts, and push vehicles to their limit across a growing set of maps.

🚗Multiple Vehicles

Each with its own handling profile.

↻Drift System

Momentum-based cornering, tuned for skill ceiling.

🔥Burnouts

Build heat before the light goes green.

⚡Launch Burnouts

Perfect the start for a real speed edge.

🚀NOS

A boost system for the moments that matter.

⏱Time Attack

Race the clock and your own best lap.

🎯Free Play

Open sessions with no pressure, no timer.

📶LAN Multiplayer

Host or join local races with friends.

🗺Multiple Maps

New tracks arrive with regular updates.

VelocityVentures — Multiplayer Lobby Screenshot

Mode 02

VelocityVentures

A fast-paced first-person shooter built for vertical movement. Wall-run, slide and grapple through maps designed to reward speed as much as aim.

🔫FPS Gameplay

Core first-person shooting, built from scratch.

🏃Wall Running

Keep momentum along vertical surfaces.

⬆Double Jump

Extra air control for tighter movement lines.

🛹Sliding

Close distance fast, or duck under fire.

🪝Grapple Gun

Pull yourself across the map in an instant.

🌀Teleport Gun

A short-range repositioning tool with real risk.

📶LAN Multiplayer

Host or join sessions with a code or IP.

⚔Team Deathmatch

Two teams, one objective: outscore the other.

🎯Free For All

Every player for themselves.

⛰King Of The Hill

Hold the zone, hold the lead.

☣Survival Rush

Outlast escalating waves of pressure.
